How to Get NPK Values?

The crop prediction system works accurately when you enter the correct NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium) values. These values are obtained from a Soil Health Card or a Soil Test Report provided by government or private soil testing labs.

This soil report shows important parameters like NPK levels, pH value, organic carbon, and overall nutrient status. AgroSmart uses these values to determine which crop is best suited for your soil. These detailed soil parameters ensure that the prediction model can provide the most accurate crop suggestions for your field.
